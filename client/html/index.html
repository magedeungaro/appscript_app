Ordering Platform

Conceptual application that shows many aspects of mini-apps I develop on a daily basis to improve workflows.
Developed using Appscript, Pug, Sass, Materialize Css, Parcel and my SVGs were made with BoxySvg.

Why use Appscript?

Many organizations use Google Forms to organize processes. Appscript offers a way to customize many of the Google Services, including Forms itself. But why not create something even more customized with Google Scripts?
