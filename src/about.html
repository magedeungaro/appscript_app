About this

Conceptual application that shows many aspects of mini-apps I developed on a daily basis to improve workflows.
Developed using Appscript, Typescript, Pug, Sass, Materialize CSS, Parcel and the SVGs were made with BoxySvg.

Why use Appscript?

Many organizations use Google Forms to organize processes. Appscript offers a way to customize many of the Google Services, including Forms itself. But why not create something even more customized with Google Scripts?
